Journey

Dalton Turner

Overview

Journey is a 2D platformer involving a professor that explores caves and defeats enemies within them. The original idea came from the book: "Journey to the Center of the Earth" by Jules Verne.

Game Mechanics

Combat

Defeating enemies involves jumping on top on them while not getting damaged by them


Climbing

Rope is used to climb up walls too high to jump


Health

Enemies have a chance to drop hearts, which can heal the player

Controls

Left and Right Arrows: Movement

Space: Jump

E: Deploy Rope

R: Restart Game

Esc: Pause Game

Levels

Level 1

Easiest level with a few enemies


Level 2

Spiders are introduced and there are more enemies


Level 3

Player navigates a lava river before fighting the final boss
